Welcome to Riverside Burger Bash! Located alongside the Mississippi River in downtown La Crosse, Wisconsin, the restaurant is a great place for a family outing. We are famous for our burgers. Our options include the Bacon Bomb, the Bash Deluxe, and the River Raft burgers.

Other meal options include our chicken sandwhiches and salads. In addition to burgers, we serve a variety of beverages and appetizers. Some of our appetizers include french fries, jalapeno poppers, and Wisconsin cheese curds. We serve many different drinks including margaritas, soda, and our classic mint milkshakes.
